Understanding Wagering Requirements

The single most important concept to grasp when dealing with any casino promotion is the wagering requirement, also known as a playthrough or rollover requirement. This condition applies to nearly every bonus and determines how many times you must bet the bonus amount (or winnings from free spins) before the funds convert into real, withdrawable money. For example, if you win $20 from your free spins and the wagering requirement is 40x, you’ll need to place a total of $800 (20 × 40) in bets before those funds become available for withdrawal. Always read the bonus terms and conditions carefully to understand this multiplier, as it varies from one promotion to another and directly affects how easily you can cash out your winnings.

Furthermore, not all games contribute equally to meeting these requirements. Typically, slot games contribute 100% of your bet amount towards the playthrough. However, table games like blackjack and roulette, due to their lower house edge, might only contribute 5% or 10%, or sometimes not at all. This means if you bet $1 on a slot, the full $1 counts, but if you bet $1 on blackjack, only $0.05 might count towards your requirement. Therefore, the most efficient way to clear a bonus is almost always by playing eligible slot machines.
